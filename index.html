Biography

I am an Assistant Professor in Economics and Social Data Science at University of Copenhagen. I am the Head of Studies for MSc Social Data Science from 2019-2021.

The majority of my research focuses on education research, using methods from economics and data science. Some focus area within education is school choice, peer effects, use of digital technology, and prediction for intervention. Other research topics include using tools from econometrics and data science to investigate human behavior more broadly.
